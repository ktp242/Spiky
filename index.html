SPIKY is an interactive/kinetic scuplture inspired by animals with spikes to protect themselves. With the sensors and motors, SPIKY is able to react to the environment, so that if someone's approacing it, the spikes will immediately be raise up to scare off visitors. The sculpture is an experiment for both simplifying the original appereance of these animals and marrying my personal view.

Move your mouse to hover the title(if you haven't), to see what SPIKY can do.
